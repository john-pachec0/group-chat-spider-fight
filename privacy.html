Privacy Policy

Group Chat Spider Fight

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Group Chat Spider Fight is a social game where friends photograph spiders, vote on stats, and battle them. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

The short version: We only collect what's needed to make the game work. We don't sell your data or show you ads.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Display Name: The name you choose to show to your group members
  • Email Address: Used for authentication and account recovery

Spider Photos

  • Photos you upload are stored securely and only visible to members of your group
  • We do not analyze, sell, or share your photos outside your group
  • Photos are stored using Firebase Storage with security rules restricting access

Game Data

  • Your votes on spider stats
  • Group membership information
  • Battle history and statistics
  • Chat messages within your groups

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To enable gameplay features (voting, battles, chat)
  • To show your spider photos and stats to your group members
  • To send you notifications about game events (if enabled)

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not show advertisements
  • We do not track your behavior for analytics purposes
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except Firebase for hosting)

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages your login credentials securely
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores game data (votes, groups, battles)
  • Firebase Storage: Stores spider photos

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Firebase complies with industry security standards.

Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Spider photos remain in your group until deleted by the owner
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Group Chat Spider Fight is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
