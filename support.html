Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a group?

Tap the "Create Group" button on the home screen, give your group a name, and share the invite code with your friends. Groups work best with 4-8 people.

How do I join a group?

Get an invite code from a friend who's already in a group, then tap "Join Group" and enter the code.

Why are my spider's stats showing as "???"

Stats are hidden until all group members have voted. This keeps voting fair and makes the reveal more exciting! Once everyone votes, you'll see a dramatic stat reveal.

Can I be in multiple groups?

Yes! You can be a member of multiple groups. Each group has its own spiders, battles, and Hall of Fame.

How are battles decided?

Battles are auto-simulated based on your spider's stats. Each stat contributes to different battle abilities. The spider with better overall performance wins!

Can I delete a spider?

Yes, you can delete spiders you've uploaded. Go to the spider's detail page and look for the delete option. Note: this cannot be undone.

What if someone uploads something that's not a spider?

Group members can report photos that aren't spiders. If enough members report it, the photo will be flagged for review.

Is my data private?

Yes! Your spider photos and game data are only visible to members of your groups. We don't sell data or show ads. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at spiderfight.app@gmail.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data.
